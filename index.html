Our kitchen story The Heart Behind Every Dabba Open this note to know why every meal is served with care.

Some stories are written with courage.

In 2002, after losing her husband, our founder's life changed overnight. A homemaker became the sole pillar of her family, finding the strength to step into the workforce and build a new future for her children.

For 22 years, she served with honesty, compassion and dedication at the Punjab Human Rights Commission, earning the respect and love of everyone around her.

Now, she begins a new journey with The Daily Dabba. Cooking has always been her language of love. What once brought comfort to her family is now shared with yours through every meal we prepare.

The Daily Dabba is not just about food. It is about serving every meal with the same care, warmth and love that helped one family through life's toughest days. Welcome to our family. Read the full story
